What information does Wave Farm collect about me?

In simple terms, there are two categories of information Wave Farm may collect about you: user-provided information and site usage information.

User-provided information can be used to identify you as an individual. This includes names, physical/postal addresses, email addresses, telephone numbers, credit/debit card information, and social media profile information. This information is only collected when you have consented to share it, typically when you:

  • ---Donate or otherwise conduct a transaction online with us;
  • ---Create an account with us;
  • ---Subscribe to receive emails or newsletters from us;
  • ---Complete surveys with us;
  • ---Register to communicate on a forum with us; and
  • ---Submit comments or questions to us.

    • If you wish to opt out of any of these services you are free to do so at any time, primarily by closing any accounts you have established with us. For newsletters or other promotional communication, simply send us an email with the subject line “Unsubscribe” to info@wavefarm.org. Please note that it may take us a few days to process your request, and that we may still contact you if you engage in non-promotional correspondence from us, such as making a donation or participating in a forum.

    Our servers automatically collect site usage information when you visit Wave Farm’s online platforms. This information includes your IP address (and the relevant domain name of which it is a part), the type of device you are using to connect to us, information about your browser and operating system settings, information about your usage behavior, such as how long you spend on our platforms, what you click on or download, search terms you use, and link-referral information.

    We or our service providers and partners may also utilize common tracking technologies when you visit or interact with us, including cookies, pixel tags, and browser fingerprinting. We use cookies and other tracking technologies to collect information in order to improve our services for you. If you choose to later opt out of using cookies, you may always use the settings of your browser; however, this may negatively impact your experience on our website or your access to certain functionality.

    Wave Farm also has a presence on many third-party social networking and publishing platforms, and we incorporate some of those features into our own online home. Through these features, we may receive some user-provided and site usage information about you. This Privacy Policy applies to that information which we collect and keep from these features, but keep in mind that third party platforms have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to examine for more information on how they collect data and what your choices are in regards to data collected about you. .

    Wave Farm may embed links, banners, widgets or other content from third parties into our own online home. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that third party website. We are not responsible for how these third parties may collect and utilize information about you if you interact with it. We strongly advise you to review the Privacy Policy of every site you visit and encourage you to read their privacy policies as well[1] .

    We strive to protect the security and integrity of all user-provided information collected on Wave Farm’s online platforms, but we cannot guarantee that your information will be absolutely safe during transmission or while stored on our system. If you correspond with us via email, you should be aware that your information is susceptible to interception by a third party while in transit.
