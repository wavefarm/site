WGXC 90.7-FM NEWSROOM

WGXC's Newroom (http://newsroom.wgxc.org) includes local news summaries, breaking news, features, local audio, video, analysis, music, and links to events in Greene and Columbia counties, NY. The Newsroom contains scripts and sounds for WGXC radio hosts to play on the WGXC Morning Show or WGXC Afternoon Show.

WGXC News airs at 5 a.m., 7 a.m., 9 a.m., 12 p.m. (noon), 4 p.m., 5 p.m., 12 a.m. (midnight) on 90.7-FM and wgxc.org
