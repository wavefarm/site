The Media Arts Assistance Fund supports electronic media and film organizations, as well as individual artists, in all regions of New York State. The Fund provides unique and critical support with a focus on sustainability and public engagement. Please join our email list to ensure you receive up-to-date information about this program.

Direct your questions to mag@wavefarm.org.
