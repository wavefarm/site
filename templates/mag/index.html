ABOUT MEDIA ARTS GRANTS

Media Arts Grants programs include the New York Media Arts Map and the Media Arts Assistance Fund (MAAF), which support electronic media and film organizations, as well as individual artists, in all regions of New York State through a Regrant Partnership with NYSCA, Electronic Media and Film, as well as fiscal sponsorship.

MEDIA ARTS ASSISTANCE FUND (MAAF)

The Media Arts Assistance Fund supports electronic media and film organizations, as well as individual artists, in all regions of New York State. The Fund provides unique and critical support with a focus on sustainability and public engagement. MAAF is made possible by a Regrant Partnership with the New York State Council on the Arts, Electronic Media and Film Program. Please direct all questions to mag@wavefarm.org.

MAAF For Artists

Grants to media artists support the distribution and exhibition of works in all genres of time-based and moving image media, including emergent technology. Funding assists artists in making recently completed works available to public audiences.

MAAF For Organizations

Grants to media arts organizations address capacity and technology needs, providing funds to hire outside consultants in three areas critical for organizations, who face unique challenges in a field of rapid technology development.

NEW YORK MEDIA ARTS MAP

NY Media Arts Map

In order to strengthen and promote the Media Arts throughout New York State, Wave Farm will execute a significant overhaul of nymediaartsmap.org launching spring 2015. A vital connecting point for cultural organizations and the general public interested in electronic art, media, and film statewide, the new Map will dynamically profile New York Media Arts Organizations and also feature a funding calendar, consultant directory, convening archive, and discussion group. The New York Media Arts Map is made possible through a Regrant Partnership between Wave Farm and the New York State Council on the Arts, Electronic Media and Film Program. Please direct all questions to mag@wavefarm.org.



FISCAL SPONSORSHIP

In addition to the opportunities and resources above, Wave Farm provides fiscal sponsorship to artists on a case-by-case basis for projects that are aligned with Wave Farm's organizational mission. Inquiries should be directed to mag@wavefarm.org well in advance of any related grant deadline.
