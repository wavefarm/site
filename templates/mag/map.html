NYS MEDIA ARTS MAP

In order to strengthen and promote the Media Arts throughout New York State, Wave Farm will execute a significant overhaul of nymediaartsmap.org launching spring 2015. A vital connecting point for cultural organizations and the general public interested in electronic art, media, and film statewide, the new Map will dynamically profile New York Media Arts Organizations and also feature a funding calendar, consultant directory, convening archive, and discussion group. The New York Media Arts Map is made possible through a Regrant Partnership between Wave Farm and the New York State Council on the Arts, Electronic Media and Film Program.
